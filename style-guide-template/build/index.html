Style Guide 1.0

Inner Wayfaring

Our site reflects one of the few escapes from seclusion a bookshelf and place to read provide during this home quarantine period. From the familiarity of our bookshelves we can freely explore new ideas in various situations. Jumping into a new book tends to construct a mental shelter with which we draw peace though we might be surrounded by world of chaos. As a Zen Garden should, we feel our site creates a harmonious balance between the outward exploration within the mind while infused yet with an awareness of our physical reality.
